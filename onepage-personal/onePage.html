About Me

ZURI IVY

Zuri Ivy is a creative, high-energy photographer producing premium-quality photos in studio settings. She is dedicated to creating memories that last a lifetime while driving sales and studio profits. She manages all facets of photo shoots including scheduling, designing sets, shooting, photo editing, collaborating with clients and closing add-on sales. She maintains strong digital/technical skills and uses Adobe Photoshop Lightroom CC daily.

Zuri graduated from the ABC Institute of Nigeria in 2013 where she obtained a Bachelor’s degree in Photography. She is a proud member of the Professional Photographers of Nigeria (PPN). She specializes majorly in wedding, portrait and landscape photography. As a beneficiary of different honors and awards, Zuri has shown great strides in the photography industry and continues to do.
